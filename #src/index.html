ABOUT US

California Psychological Services, Inc. is a comprehensive agency providing an impressive breadth of direct mental health services to a diverse range of clientele within the greater Los Angeles area. We provide individual, couples, family, and group therapy, as well as comprehensive psychological assessments, testing, and consultation services. The cornerstone of our practice constitutes empirically-based approaches, an acknowledgement of the fundamental mind-body connection, and strong ethics rooted in a social justice sensibility.

Employment & Training

California Psychological Services, Inc. is a prospering group practice with locations throughout greater Los Angeles. We were founded on the principles of providing access to quality mental health services to individuals despite systemic obstacles. Our company culture prioritizes balance, self-care, flexibility, and independence. We celebrate diversity in alignment with our strong ethics rooted in a social justice sensibility. The cornerstone of our practice constitutes empirically-based approaches, an acknowledgement of the fundamental mind-body connection, and a holistic approach to wellness. Training Opportunities

CPS provides pre-doctoral practicum and internship opportunities, as well as post-doctoral psychological assistant fellowships. Although we are currently an approved training site with The Chicago School of Professional Psychology for the applied clinical and forensic psychology programs, we do accept students from various graduate schools. Our training model is designed to strengthen your therapeutic clinical skills, assessment and diagnostic abilities, and developmentally foster your autonomy as a private practice clinician. Under supervision from licensed psychologists, we ground our training in a psychodynamic framework and strengths-based relational approach in order to provide you an environment conducive to growth.
