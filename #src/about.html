about us

California Psychological Services, Inc. is a comprehensive agency providing an impressive breadth of direct mental health services to a diverse range of clientele within the greater Los Angeles area. We provide individual, couples, family, and group therapy, as well as comprehensive psychological assessments, testing, and consultation services. The cornerstone of our practice constitutes empirically-based approaches, an acknowledgement of the fundamental mind-body connection, and strong ethics rooted in a social justice sensibility.

our approch

We provide psychological services to an ethnically-diverse clientele hailing from Los Angeles natives to global citizens. We serve children ages 6+, pre-teens, adolescents, transitional-aged youth, young adults, middle aged adults, and late-stage adults, who struggle with mental health disorders, substance abuse, and co-occurring conditions. Our services also extend to sexual and gender minorities, including those considering/enduring/completed with transitioning, nonbinary-identified, gay/bi/pan/demisexual, or those who generally identify as queer. Non-“traditional” family configurations are also welcomed for family therapy.

our vision

Our practice is in-network with most major insurance companies. In addition, we provide low sliding scale rates for those who do not have coverage, in stride with our social justice mentality. Lastly, we see people who would like to incorporate religion and/or spirituality into their traditional psychotherapy, and we encourage the addition of other healing modalities resonant to our clients in service of achieving greater progress towards their therapeutic goals.
